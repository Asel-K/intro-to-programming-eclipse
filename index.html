About me

I am a life-long learner & beginner developer based in Austin, TX.

My name is Asel Karagazieva. I am a student at Code the Dream and mom of two toddlers. My main goal is to become a professional in a completely new path for me, which I start from zero and coming from other part of the world.

Today, I am passionate about Front End Development, area where coding and design work together. My main goal is to create clean, unique and functional websites, designs that are eye-catching, designs that work.

My interests, however, extend beyond the web and I also enjoy taking photos and retouching.
